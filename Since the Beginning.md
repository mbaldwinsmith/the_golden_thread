I waited since the beginning for you,  
From speaking light into being,  
I counted down every second until  
I could tell you how loved you are!

I know every hair on your head,  
My breath is in every cell of yours.  
I have never regretted making you  
And to the end of time, I never will.

Every one of my beloved prophets spoke  
So that you would open your eyes to me  
And lift up your voice in my chorus.  
You were made with a song to sing to me.

I sing in every blade of grass and whisper  
In the wind of the hills and dales.  
I cry out in the song of the morning lark  
And laugh in the gurgling of streams.

I am Alpha and Omega, beginning and end.  
Morning Star and the dark shade of evening  
My being is the being in all things.  
I hide in plain sight behind every door.

Knock for Me and I shall always answer.  
Cry out in the silence of the night  
And I will listen to every word you utter.  
Ask in My name, for I delight in gifts.

Treasure what I love in your heart  
And you will reap hundredfold what you sow.  
Join my dance, the song of the harvest,  
And New Eden will throng with thanksgiving.

Every soul a flower all adding sweet scent  
And lending the hue of their petals  
To a timeless dance under my breath.  
Every one treasured since the beginning.
