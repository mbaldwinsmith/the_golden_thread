My peace I give to you, peace be with you!  
Deepest Shalom, beyond earthly knowing.

In grass, sunlight and birdsong, a Paschal view.  
My yoke is easy, my burden light, overflowing.

Knock and I shall answer, seek and find!  
Keep My commandments, abide and simply be.

Follow Me, become fishers of another kind,  
you will have treasure in heaven: now, follow Me.

One family, under My Father eternal, be My bride,  
know My love for you, from before you were born.

Make your burdens mine, in Me you can confide,  
Come to My Father boldly, the curtain is torn.
